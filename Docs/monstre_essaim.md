# L'Essaim / The Swarm

## Stats
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Une masse mouvante de petites créatures volantes (insectes ou parasites ailés). Elles se déplacent comme une seule entité. Couleur sombre, presque liquide. Quelques individus se détachent en vol erratique.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A dense swarm of small flying parasitic creatures forming a single moving mass in the air. Dark insectoid bodies, almost liquid in motion, individual creatures separating from the main cloud in erratic flight. The swarm has a vague humanoid or animal-like silhouette but constantly shifting.

Setting: an open dusty plain at dusk, distant blurred horizon, low light.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: charcoal black, dark grey, dusty ochre, dim purple sky, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The swarm takes up about 60% of the frame, environment visible around it.
```
