# Le Crochet / The Hook

> *« Il accroche, il tire, il dévore. »*

## Stats
- **Nom FR :** Le Crochet | **Nom EN :** The Hook
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 2 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Petite créature prédatrice, corps maigre et nerveux, dotée de griffes recourbées surdimensionnées comme des hameçons. Elle se déplace par bonds rapides. Peau noire luisante, yeux jaunes vifs. Les crochets brillent comme du métal.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A small feral predator the size of a dog, lean and wiry body, with massive curved hook-like claws on its front limbs that look like fishing hooks. Glossy black skin, bright yellow eyes. Crouched ready to leap. The metallic hooks gleam dangerously.

Setting: a rocky tundra at twilight, scattered bones, sparse dry grass, distant mountains in shadow.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: glossy black, bright yellow eyes, cold grey rocks, dusty brown, cream highlights, metallic silver on hooks. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, environment around it.
```
