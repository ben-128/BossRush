## Prompt de regeneration complete

```
Dark fantasy ink engraving illustration of a male diplomat in his
mid 50s. He is VERY TALL with LONG LEGS but takes up only 40-45%
of the image height - he must look SMALL in a VAST space, like a
Caspar David Friedrich painting. Leave LOTS of empty space above,
below, and on both sides. Broad-shouldered, slightly overweight
with a soft belly, but NOT obese, NOT short. FULL BODY visible
from head to feet with BOTH FULL BOOTS clearly visible on the
ground.

POSE: arms spread WIDE OPEN in a welcoming gesture, palms facing
up. His LEFT ARM is extended to the side holding a thick
leather-bound book with a brown/sienna cover - the book rests FLAT
on his open upturned palm like he is presenting it, supported from
below by his gloved hand. His RIGHT ARM is extended to the other
side, open palm up, empty. A dagger hangs at his belt - the dagger
pommel has a brown/sienna gemstone.

He wears a rich dark leather cape with fur trim over a fine doublet.
Brown/sienna leather BOOTS clearly colored brown, not black.
Brown/sienna leather GLOVES clearly colored brown, covering all
fingers completely.

Style of Gustave Dore woodcut engraving with heavy crosshatching,
dramatic chiaroscuro. SERIOUS, DARK, REALISTIC tone - NOT cartoon,
NOT illustration for children, NOT whimsical, NOT caricature. This
is a grim dark fantasy world. Black ink on a flat smooth light COOL
grey-beige background - NOT warm, NOT yellow, NOT sepia. No paper
grain, no fiber texture.

HATCHING STYLE IS CRITICAL: the hatching must be ORGANIC, IRREGULAR,
and HAND-MADE looking. Varying thickness, varying spacing, slightly
wobbly lines like a real hand-carved woodcut. NOT regular, NOT
parallel, NOT geometric, NOT clean. Contour lines must BLEND INTO
the hatching, not be separate outlines. NO "outline + fill" look.
Overall ink coverage around 50%.

High contrast, limited palette: black ink with selective brown/sienna
accent on: the book cover, the gloves, the boots, the dagger pommel.
These brown elements must be CLEARLY VISIBLE as colored brown, not
dark or lost in black ink. NO color on the background.

BACKGROUND: a vast medieval great hall seen from INSIDE with DEEP
PERSPECTIVE - long corridor of stone pillars receding into the
distance, vaulted gothic ceiling arches overhead, large stained glass
window glowing at the far end, stone floor with perspective lines,
tapestries on distant walls, banquet table in middle distance. The
hall must feel GRAND and DEEP. Render with SPARSE bold IRREGULAR
strokes only. Lots of empty light grey-beige space.

Sharp detailed face rendered PRIMARILY in black ink hatching like the rest of the body.
A very faint warm skin tone is visible as a WASH underneath the
hatching - like an old engraving lightly hand-tinted with watercolor.
The BLACK INK HATCHING must DOMINATE the face, the skin color is
barely perceptible underneath. The face must NOT look more realistic
or painted than the rest of the illustration. Strongly ASYMMETRIC - crooked nose
bending hard left, one eye noticeably smaller, scar through right
eyebrow. Salt-and-pepper patchy beard, receding hairline NOT bald,
double chin, deep laugh lines, age spots, pockmarks. EXPRESSION:
genuinely warm and likeable - a big friendly smile, crinkled eyes,
instinctively trustworthy. But something slightly OFF - the eyes a
little too sharp behind the warmth. The face is the SHARPEST most
detailed area.

Foreground: iron candelabra, wine goblet on stone step.

Printmaking aesthetic, hand-carved feel. Landscape orientation,
3:2 aspect ratio.
```
