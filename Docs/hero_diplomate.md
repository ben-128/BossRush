## Prompt de regeneration complete

```
Dark fantasy ink engraving illustration of a male diplomat in his
mid 50s, tall and HEAVYSET - a large, well-fed man with a thick
round body, double chin, broad shoulders padded with fat. FULL BODY
visible from head to feet, both feet on the ground. The character
occupies about 60-70% of the image height, vertically centered.

POSE: arms spread WIDE OPEN in a welcoming gesture, palms facing up,
like he is inviting you to a negotiation or saying "let us be
reasonable". A thick brown/sienna leather-bound book tucked under one
arm. A concealed dagger visible at his belt - the dagger pommel is
brown/sienna colored.

He wears a rich leather cape with fur trim over a fine but straining
doublet. Tall brown/sienna leather boots. Brown/sienna leather
gloves on both hands. On one hand a LARGE ORNATE RING with a
brown/sienna gemstone is worn OVER the glove.

Style of Gustave Dore woodcut engraving with heavy crosshatching,
dramatic chiaroscuro. Black ink on a flat smooth light grey-beige
background. No paper grain, no fiber texture. Plain smooth flat
light background color.

THICK SPACED OUT hatching strokes for shading, NOT fine dense
hatching - big bold woodcut strokes. Overall ink coverage should be
around 50%. IMPORTANT: the background is an INTERIOR great hall -
do NOT try to fill every wall, pillar, and surface with dense
hatching. Leave LARGE areas of plain light grey-beige showing
through. The walls and ceiling should be SUGGESTED with sparse bold
strokes, not fully rendered. Think of it like a quick bold sketch
of the room, not a detailed architectural rendering. Plenty of
empty space.

High contrast, limited palette: black ink with selective warm
brown/sienna accent ONLY on elements ON THE CHARACTER: the leather-bound book, the leather
gloves, the leather boots, the gemstone on his ring, and the dagger
pommel. NO color on the background or decor - the hall is pure black
ink and grey-beige only.

Background: vast medieval great hall, LOOSELY suggested. Soaring
vaulted ceiling hinted with a few bold arch lines. Stone pillars
suggested with simple vertical strokes. Long oak banquet table
barely visible in distance. A few tapestry shapes on walls. Large
stained glass window far end as simple outline. Stone floor with a
few perspective lines. Keep it SPARSE and OPEN - the character is
the focus, the room is just context.

Sharp detailed UGLY face of a middle-aged man, with natural realistic skin color (not ink black, not brown - just
normal caucasian skin tones with warm light). Strongly
ASYMMETRIC features - nothing lines up. Crooked aristocratic nose
bends hard to the left. One eye noticeably smaller than the other.
Thin scar across right eyebrow making it sit higher - permanent
skeptical look. Salt-and-pepper beard neatly trimmed but patchy on
one side. Receding hairline (NOT bald). Double chin. Deep laugh
lines, age spots, rough pockmarked weathered skin. EXPRESSION: false
warmth, sly smirk, one eyebrow raised - he is trying to convince you
of something and you should NOT trust him. Smug, scheming,
persuasive. The smile is calculated, the eyes are cold. The face must
be the SHARPEST most detailed area. Paint with rough thick strokes,
NOT smooth.

Foreground: ornate iron candelabra, discarded leather riding gloves
and a wine goblet on a stone step.

Printmaking aesthetic, hand-carved feel. Landscape orientation, 3:2
aspect ratio.
```

## Notes
- Le Diplomate est le hero le plus "humain" — pas de magie, pas d'armure lourde. La personnalite doit venir du VISAGE et de la POSTURE
- La couleur d'accent brun/sepia est la plus subtile — elle doit etre clairement visible sur le sceau et les blasons sinon la carte sera monochrome
- Reference de densite : le Guerrier corrige (~50%)
