# Le Tesson / The Shard

## Stats
- **PV :** 2 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Créature faite de fragments d'os et de pierre noire fusionnés ensemble, sans forme cohérente. Comme un tas de débris animés. Veines de cristal noir entre les morceaux. Se déplace par mouvements saccadés. Pas de visage clair.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A creature made of fused fragments of bone and dark stone, no coherent form, like an animated pile of debris. Black crystal corruption veins running between the pieces. Moves in jerky motions. No clear face, just shifting shards.

Setting: stony battlefield with broken weapons and shattered shields, grey overcast sky, distant mountains.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: bone cream, dark slate grey, charcoal black, deep purple corruption, cold grey sky, rust red. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, battlefield around it.
```
