# La Mâchoire / The Maw

## Stats
- **PV :** 3 | **Dégâts :** 2 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Grande créature trapue avec une gueule disproportionnée qui prend la moitié de son corps. Pattes courtes et puissantes, peau épaisse et grise. Quand elle marche, elle traîne sa mâchoire au sol. Yeux petits et sombres au-dessus de la gueule.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A large stocky creature with an oversized jaw making up half its body. Short powerful legs, thick grey hide. Drags its massive mouth along the ground as it moves. Small dark eyes above the gaping jaws filled with rows of teeth.

Setting: a desolate ashen plain, cracked dry ground, distant ruined stone pillars, overcast grey sky.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: cold grey, dark brown, charcoal black, pale ash, cream highlights, dried blood red. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 65% of the frame, environment around it.
```
