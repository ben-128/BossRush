# Nawel — Le Rempart / The Bulwark

## Identité

- **Nom :** Nawel (Mapuche : "jaguar")
- **Classe FR :** Le Rempart
- **Classe EN :** The Bulwark
- **Tribu :** Tribu du Fer — forgerons guerriers, armures lourdes
- **Rôle :** Tank, absorbe les coups, protège le groupe
- **Ethnie :** Amérindien

## Description visuelle

- Guerrier puissant, massif, musclé
- Peau cuivrée/brune foncée, traits amérindiens (pommettes hautes, nez large, mâchoire forte)
- Visage buriné, cicatrisé, asymétrique — pas beau mais puissant
- Peinture de guerre bleue sur un côté du visage
- Cheveux noirs longs attachés en arrière
- Armure mixte : plaques de fer forgé + cuir épais + os
- Cape en peau de jaguar, tête du jaguar sur une épaule
- **Bouclier rond massif** (couvre la majorité du torse) avec motifs géométriques solaires
- Massue de pierre de guerre

## Pose de référence

Stance défensive dynamique : genoux fléchis, bouclier levé devant le torse pour dévier un coup, massue tirée en arrière prête à contre-attaquer. Poids du corps vers l'avant, tension dans chaque muscle, cape fouettée par le vent.

## Couleur dominante

Deep steel blue

## Éléments portant la couleur

- Plumes bleues sur la coiffe / les épaules
- Motifs géométriques bleus sur le bouclier
- Peinture de guerre bleue sur le visage

## Palette

Deep steel blue, burnt sienna, warm ochre gold, charcoal black, dark brown, cream white

## Sous-titre verso (lore)

- **FR :** *« Tant qu'il tient debout, ils tiennent. »*
- **EN :** *« While he stands, they stand. »*

## Prompt VERSO (DALL-E, impasto, 2:3 portrait)

```
Full body character portrait, portrait vertical 2:3 format, wide shot showing full environment. A strong Indigenous American warrior in a dynamic defensive stance on a rocky cliff edge, knees bent low, holding an oversized massive round shield with bold blue geometric sun patterns covering most of his torso raised to deflect a blow, war club pulled back ready to counter-strike. His weight shifts forward, tension in every muscle, fur cloak whipping in the wind behind him. He wears a mix of forged iron plates and thick leather armor, a jaguar pelt cape caught in the wind. Deep blue war paint across one side of his face, blue feathers on his shoulders and headpiece, long black hair tied back, deep copper brown skin, strong Indigenous features, high cheekbones, broad nose, weathered and scarred face, not handsome but powerful. His eyes are locked on something coming at him.

Behind him: a vast wild landscape with jagged mountain peaks, dense jungle canopy below, and massive ancient stone ruins half-swallowed by vegetation. Dramatic sky with heavy clouds and warm golden light breaking through.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges but maintain clear shapes and defined silhouette. Colors are rich and saturated, not broken into dots or dabs: deep steel blue, burnt sienna, warm ochre gold, charcoal black, dark brown, cream white. The texture is in the surface of the paint, not in dissolving the image. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. Face must be painted with the SAME thick rough strokes as the rest of the painting. No fine detail on the face. Features suggested by bold paint slabs, not rendered smoothly. The face should NOT be more detailed than the armor or background. No smooth digital look, no photorealism, but also no abstract impressionist blur.

Character takes up about 60-70% of the frame, full body visible head to toe, environment with significant depth around and behind him.
```
