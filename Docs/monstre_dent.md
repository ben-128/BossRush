# La Dent / The Fang

> *« Il n'a gardé que sa morsure. »*

## Stats
- **Nom FR :** La Dent | **Nom EN :** The Fang
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 2 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Créature presque entièrement constituée d'une mâchoire massive. Corps rabougri sous la gueule, membres vestigiaux. Crocs énormes recourbés, gencives noires de corruption. Pas d'yeux visibles, juste de la bouche.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, close-up shot. A creature almost entirely made of a massive jaw and mouth. Stunted body underneath, vestigial limbs barely visible. Huge curved fangs, blackened gums showing corruption. No visible eyes, just teeth and a gaping maw. Strings of saliva.

Setting: a wet cave floor, dripping stalactites visible above, dim blue cave light, scattered bones.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: pale yellow ivory teeth, dark red gums, charcoal black corruption, cream skin, dim blue cave shadows. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. Disturbing but not gory. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, cave environment around it.
```
