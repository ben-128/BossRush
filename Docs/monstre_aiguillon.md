# L'Aiguillon / The Stinger

## Stats
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** ✓ (à définir)

## Description visuelle
Petite créature insectoïde, mi-scorpion mi-guêpe. Corps segmenté noir et rouge, longue queue dressée terminée par un dard luisant de venin. Six pattes acérées, mandibules.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A small insectoid creature, half scorpion half wasp. Segmented black and red body, long upright tail ending in a glistening venom-coated stinger. Six sharp legs, prominent mandibles. Posed aggressively, tail raised ready to strike.

Setting: dry cracked earth in an arid desert, scattered small stones, faint heat haze, golden hour light.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: deep black, blood red, sickly green venom highlight, dusty ochre earth, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, desert ground around it.
```
