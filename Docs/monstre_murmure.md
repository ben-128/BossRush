# Le Murmure / The Whisper

## Stats
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Petite forme spectrale, comme une silhouette d'enfant en lambeaux. Bouche immense ouverte d'où s'échappent des chuchotements visibles (volutes blanches). Yeux creux. Cheveux flottants comme dans l'eau.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A small ghostly figure like a child-shaped silhouette in tattered shroud. Huge gaping mouth releasing visible whispers as white wisps. Hollow black eye sockets. Hair floating as if underwater. Pale translucent body.

Setting: an ancient stone shrine in a misty mountain pass, weathered carvings on the stones, low fog, faint candlelight from forgotten offerings.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: pale ghostly white, charcoal black eyes, cold blue mist, dim candle yellow, weathered stone grey, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. Disturbing but not gory. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, shrine around it.
```
