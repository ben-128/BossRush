# L'Ombre / The Shade

> *« Elle a perdu son corps, mais pas sa rage. »*

## Stats

## Description visuelle
Forme humanoïde sans substance, faite d'ombre liquide et de fumée noire. Pas de visage, juste deux yeux blancs lumineux. Glisse au sol sans marcher. Légèrement transparente — on voit le décor à travers.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A semi-transparent humanoid form made of liquid shadow and black smoke. No face, just two glowing white eyes. Glides along the ground without walking. The environment is faintly visible through its body.

Setting: a moonlit ruined village at night, broken walls, scattered abandoned belongings, pale blue moonlight, mist on the ground.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: charcoal black, deep purple, ghostly pale blue moonlight, cream white eyes, cold grey ruins. The shadow form must be readable but slightly transparent. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, ruined village around it.
```
