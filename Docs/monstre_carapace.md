# La Carapace / The Husk

## Stats
- **PV :** 3 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Créature lourdement blindée, comme un scarabée géant ou un tatou. Carapace épaisse couverte de plaques cornées et de pointes. Pattes courtes et puissantes. Peu visible sous son armure naturelle, juste une fente où se devinent des yeux et des mandibules.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A heavily armored creature like a giant beetle or armadillo. Thick carapace covered in horned plates and spikes. Short powerful legs. Almost nothing visible under its natural armor, just a slit where eyes and mandibles can be glimpsed. Black corrupted moss growing in the joints.

Setting: a damp jungle floor with twisted roots, mossy stones, filtered green light through dense canopy above.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: deep brown, dark green, charcoal black, mossy ochre, dim emerald light, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 65% of the frame, jungle floor around it.
```
