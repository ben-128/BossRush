## Description visuelle
Nuage de brume dense, vaguement humanoïde. Couleur grise tirant sur le vert malsain. Des formes floues de bras et de visages apparaissent puis se dissipent dans le brouillard. Ne touche pas le sol.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A dense mist cloud vaguely shaped like a humanoid figure. Grey color tinged with sickly pale green. Vague shapes of arms and faces appear and dissolve within the fog. Floating, not touching the ground.

Setting: a frozen swamp at dawn, dead twisted trees rising from icy water, pale grey sky, frost on everything, faint sunrise glow.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable for a mist creature. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: pale grey, sickly green, cold blue-white, dark frozen brown, cream highlights, dim pink dawn sky. The mist must feel solid enough to be a creature but with soft edges. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, swamp around it.
```
