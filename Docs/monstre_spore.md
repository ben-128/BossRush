# La Spore / The Spore

## Stats
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** ✓ (à définir)

## Description visuelle
Champignon parasitique de la taille d'une tête humaine. Chapeau bombé, sombre, couvert de pores noirs qui semblent respirer. Une fente s'ouvre et libère un nuage de spores toxiques quand il est touché. Tige courte enracinée dans une chair étrange.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, close-up shot. A parasitic fungal pod the size of a human head. Domed dark cap covered in pulsing black pores. A vertical slit opens releasing a cloud of toxic spores. Short stem rooted in strange flesh-like growth on the ground. Sickly luminescent green-yellow on the underside.

Setting: a dark forest floor with rotting leaves and dead twigs, mossy stones, dim filtered light.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: dark purple, sickly green-yellow, charcoal black, deep brown, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, forest floor around it.
```
