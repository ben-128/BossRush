# Le Limon / The Slime

> *« Il digère lentement ce qui s'y prend. »*

## Stats
- **Nom FR :** Le Limon | **Nom EN :** The Slime
- **PV :** 2 | **Dégâts :** 2 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Masse visqueuse rampant au sol, couleur verdâtre tirant sur le brun. Translucide — on voit des fragments d'os et d'objets digérés à l'intérieur. Forme constamment changeante. Laisse une traînée corrosive derrière lui.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A viscous slime mass crawling on the ground, greenish-brown color, partially translucent showing bones and digested objects inside. Constantly shifting shape. Leaves a corrosive trail behind it. Bubbles forming and popping on its surface.

Setting: a damp underground cavern with stalagmites, slick wet stone floor, dim greenish phosphorescent moss on the walls.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: sickly green, dark brown, pale yellow translucency, charcoal black bone fragments, dim phosphorescent green light, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, cavern around it.
```
