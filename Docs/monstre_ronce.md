## Description visuelle
Plante carnivore mobile, masse de tiges épineuses noires et tordues qui se déplace en rampant. Quelques fleurs rouge sang au sommet. Les épines sont longues et pointues. Pas de tête claire — c'est juste un buisson agressif.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. A mobile carnivorous plant, a mass of black twisted thorny vines crawling on the ground. A few blood-red flowers at its top. Long sharp thorns covering everything. No clear head, just an aggressive bush of writhing tendrils. Small bones tangled in the vines from previous victims.

Setting: a dense dark forest with thick undergrowth, fallen mossy logs, dim green-tinted light, ancient trees in the background.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: deep black, blood red flowers, dark forest green, mossy brown, charcoal black thorns, cream highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 65% of the frame, forest around it.
```
