# Caicai

## Identité

- **Nom :** Caicai
- **Nom complet (référence) :** Caicai Vilu
- **Difficulté :** Moyen
- **Culture d'origine :** Mapuche (Chili/Argentine)
- **Référence mythologique :** Caicai Vilu — serpent de mer titanesque qui inonde le monde et provoque tsunamis et tremblements de terre dans la mythologie mapuche

## Description visuelle

Serpent de mer titanesque, corps interminable couvert d'écailles bleu-noir luisantes, dos crêté d'épines coralliennes. Tête massive aux mâchoires immenses, dents recourbées comme des crochets. Yeux blancs sans pupille. Quand il bat sa queue, des vagues immenses se forment. L'eau autour de lui devient noire et tourbillonne. Sa peau est couverte de bernacles, d'algues et de coquillages. Corruption visible : des cristaux noirs poussent entre les écailles, comme des tumeurs.

## Stats

- **PV :** 9 + 4 par joueur (17/21/25/29)
- **Dégâts :** 2
- **Invocations :** 1
- **Passif :** *Marée noire* — Les attaques à distance contre le boss infligent 1 dégât de moins (min 1).
- **Action spéciale :** Une vague immense déferle : tous les héros subissent 2 dégâts.

## Sous-titre verso (lore)

- **FR :** *« Les vagues s'écartaient pour lui. Maintenant elles fuient. »*
- **EN :** *« Waves once parted for him. Now they flee. »*

## Prompt VERSO (DALL-E, impasto, 2:3 portrait)

```
Portrait vertical 2:3 format, wide shot showing full creature and environment. A titanic sea serpent emerging from black churning waters. Endless coiling body covered in glossy blue-black scales, spined coral ridges along its back. Massive head with huge jaws and curved hook-like teeth. Pupil-less white eyes. Skin encrusted with barnacles, seaweed, and shells. Black crystal corruption growing between scales like tumors. Tail slamming the water, creating massive waves.

Setting: a dark stormy ocean at night, tall black cliffs in the background, crashing waves, dark clouds, distant lightning illuminating the scene briefly, foam and spray everywhere.

IMPORTANT STYLE: This painting is made of VISIBLE INDIVIDUAL THICK PAINT STROKES, like a Leonid Afremov painting or Van Gogh's Starry Night. Each stroke is a separate long thick ribbon of oil paint, clearly distinct from the next stroke. The paint looks like toothpaste squeezed in long lines onto the canvas. You should be able to count individual strokes one by one. Strokes have different sizes, directions, and thicknesses — never uniform. Some strokes are big and thick, others smaller. Strokes overlap chaotically, some areas have empty spaces between them where canvas shows. The paint builds form through deliberate directional lines, not blended smoothly. The entire image (character/creature AND background) must have the same thick paint stroke technique — not smoother on the figure. Palette: deep midnight blue, charcoal black, cold teal, pale grey-white foam, dark green algae, cream highlights. Palette knife and thick brush technique. Dramatic lighting with strong light source creating highlights and shadows, warm rim light on the figure.

Forbidden: uniform texture, grain filter effect, smooth painting, evenly distributed brushwork, digital painting with impasto filter, AI-style neat strokes, smooth character on textured background.

The creature takes up about 60-70% of the frame, body visible coiling out of water, environment with significant depth around it.
```
