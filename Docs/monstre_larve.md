# La Larve / The Grub

## Stats
- **PV :** 1 | **Dégâts :** 1 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Petite larve parasitaire blanche/grisâtre, corps segmenté et mou, sans yeux, avec une bouche circulaire bordée de petites dents. Se déplace en se contractant. Peau translucide laissant voir des veines noires (corruption).

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, close-up shot. A small parasitic grub on dark earth. Pale grey-white segmented body, soft and squishy, eyeless, with a circular mouth ringed by small teeth. Translucent skin showing dark corrupted veins inside. Curling and contracting on the ground.

Setting: dark damp earth with dead leaves and small pebbles, blurred wild background.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: pale cream, sickly grey, dark brown earth, charcoal black corrupted veins. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 60% of the frame, environment around it.
```
