# Le Rampant / The Crawler

> *« Il suit ta trace depuis le premier jour. »*

## Stats
- **Nom FR :** Le Rampant | **Nom EN :** The Crawler
- **PV :** 2 | **Dégâts :** 2 | **Effet :** non

## Description visuelle
Créature au corps allongé qui rampe au ras du sol, comme un mille-pattes géant ou un lézard sans pattes apparentes. Corps couvert d'écailles sombres luisantes. Tête plate avec une mâchoire qui s'ouvre verticalement. Plusieurs paires de petits yeux rouges.

## Prompt visuel (DALL-E, impasto, 1:1)

```
Square format, mid shot. An elongated creature crawling low on the ground, like a giant centipede or a legless lizard. Body covered in dark glossy scales. Flat head with vertical opening jaws. Multiple pairs of small red eyes. Body segments bristling with small spines.

Setting: a sandy desert at night, scattered rocks, sparse dead bushes, pale moonlight, dunes in the background.

Style: thick impasto oil painting with visible palette knife strokes and paint texture, but NOT impressionism. Keep forms solid and readable. Chunky paint application with visible ridges. Colors: dark glossy black, deep brown, blood red eyes, pale cold sand, charcoal shadows, cream moonlight highlights. The texture is in the surface of the paint. Think thick paint on a solid drawing. No smooth digital rendering. No photorealism.

The creature takes up about 65% of the frame, desert around it.
```
